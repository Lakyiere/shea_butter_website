A better way to revitalize Shea Butter

The NAFAME SHEABUTTER PROCESSING CENTER regenerates raw sheanuts to industrial cosmetics and pharmaceutical Products.

Who we are

NAFAME SHEABUTTER PROCESSING CENTER is a firm
specialized in the production, processing and sales of shea butter
and other related products in Ghana.

We started Nafame Sheabutter Processing Center with a goal:

To provide a substitute to the high rise oil cost and also to fill the
vacuum within the failing Shea Industry because of the demand
of shea products by other Industries.
